Transferred Patient Appointment System

Transferred patient appointment system is system that allows patient to request for an appointment online without going to the hospital to book for an appointment only. this system will improve the productivity of the hospitals ,reduce patient waste of money and time also it will reduces chaos at the hospital.

Together with the University of Rwanda Administration,Ministry of Health, we have sets the policies and standards for a patient who wants to request for an appointment, including Insurance and tranfer from health centre or other hospitals.

Appointment Process

The Appointment process from the time you request an appointment to the time you get treated

1 - Request Appointment

After reaching to the home page of the system click on the request appointment tab



2 - Fill form

after clicking on the request appointment you will fill the given page to get an appointment

3 - Getting Message

After requesting the patient will receive message of comfirmation on the appointment




4 - Getting treatment

After getting the message of comfirmation the patient will come to the hospital for the treatment.
